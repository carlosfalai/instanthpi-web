Privacy / Confidentialité

Last updated 2026-07-04 · Applies to instanthpi.ai and app.instanthpi.ai

Carlos Faviel Font — info@instanthpi.ai

What we collect — and what we deliberately don't keep

  • AI Council services (app.instanthpi.ai): what you upload is processed in memory only, entirely on AWS Bedrock under a signed HIPAA Business Associate Agreement. Your report is emailed to you alone; then every copy of your data is discarded. Nothing is stored, no human reviews or receives your content, nothing is used for AI training. The only remnant is a SHA-256 fingerprint of the delivery email, kept so the authenticity of a report can be verified.
  • Email address: used once, to deliver what you purchased.
  • Payments: handled entirely by Stripe. Card data never touches our servers. Stripe's records (amount, receipt, your email) exist as required for payments and disputes.
  • This website: static pages; standard hosting logs (IP, page, time) are kept briefly by our hosting providers (Netlify, AWS).
  • No advertising trackers, no analytics profiles, no sale of personal information — ever.

Where processing happens

AI processing runs on AWS servers in the United States (us-east-1) inside the BAA boundary. By using the services you consent to this communication of information outside Québec; its sensitivity is protected by the zero-storage design above.

Your rights (Loi 25 / PIPEDA)

You may ask for access, rectification, or deletion of personal information, withdraw consent, or complain about our handling of it — write to the Privacy Officer at info@instanthpi.ai. Because of the zero-storage design, in most cases there is nothing on file to produce or delete beyond the payment record held by Stripe. You may also address the Commission d'accès à l'information du Québec.

Confidentiality incidents

Any incident involving personal information is assessed, logged in an incident register, and — where there is a risk of serious injury — reported to the persons concerned and the Commission d'accès à l'information, as Loi 25 requires.
