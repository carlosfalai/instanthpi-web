How the healthcare blockchain works

A human-led revision layer on AI-driven systems — everyone gets paid, everyone is accountable, and the record is public.

1 · NodesAnyone can host care

People stand up their own InstantHPI versions — their models, their combinations — reachable by Telegram or their own site. They offer consultations at the price they choose. The free bot kit on GitHub is the starter node.

2 · VerificationPhysicians rate the work

Verified physicians with active licenses review and rate node AI consensuses: proof of work, validity, corrections, comments. Physicians get paid for verification; node hosts earn income from consults. Reputation accrues to both.

3 · The public recordPerformance is published

Node and model performance — ratings, corrections, track record — is followed and published openly on instanthpi.ai. You can see which nodes to trust before you spend a dollar.

4 · The purposeProtection for mankind

Not prescriptions — AI cannot prescribe. This is the proof of concept: refining the technology until it earns trust, so that no wealthy minority can ever become the exclusive possessor of medical knowledge and take away people's autonomy over their own health. The power stays with people; the verification stays with licensed physicians.
