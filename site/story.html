Why We're Doing This

3.5 billion people have no access to healthcare. A single AI consultation costs $0.003. For $1, we can serve 333 patients. For $100, 33,000. The technology is live on Telegram right now — free, in every language. The only barrier left is reach. This page documents our effort to remove that barrier.
