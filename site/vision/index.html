InstantHPI ships free healthcare tools. Which ones come next is not our call — it's yours. Post what you want to exist. Fund what you want to see. We build the leaderboard, top-down, and report what every dollar did.

The rules

Post an idea: $1. The dollar activates your idea and puts it on the board.

Support an idea: donate $1 or more. Every donation is a vote — the board ranks by dollars raised.

100% of your donation, net of payment-processing fees, is allocated to this idea. Each idea's total and spending are reported publicly.

Fee honesty: card processing costs about $0.63 on a $1 charge; larger amounts lose proportionally less. Totals update hourly.
