InstantHPI

The flagship project. A HIPAA-compliant medical intake platform that uses AI to transform how patients and doctors interact. Built for a real Quebec medical clinic. Five panels, one mission: make healthcare documentation instant.

📥

Inbox

Spruce Health conversations, patient messages, incoming faxes

📋

History

Full patient thread, past visits, lab results, medications

⏳

Queue

60-second auto-send countdown, triage levels P1-P5, urgency scoring

🤖

AI Panel

Claude-powered SOAP notes, referral letters, HPI summaries in French

📝

Templates

SOAP notes, referrals, prescriptions, follow-up templates

What It Does

  • Patients answer 10 AI-generated follow-up questions about their symptoms
  • AI generates a complete French medical HPI summary with patient confirmation
  • Doctors get pre-written SOAP notes, referral letters, and triage scores
  • Integrates directly with Spruce Health for real patient messaging
  • All AI runs on AWS Bedrock (HIPAA-compliant under signed BAA)
  • Built with React + Vite + Tailwind, Express + TypeScript, AWS RDS PostgreSQL
Currently in active development — not yet publicly available. Powering a real clinic in Quebec.
